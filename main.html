Welcome to thot_experiment's mood board thingy, drag or paste images anywhere to place them

keybinds

  • G + Drag: Hold G to grab and move an image.
  • G + Scroll / S + Drag: Scale image.
  • X: Delete hovered image.
  • Click & Drag: Drag the board to navigate.
  • Scroll Wheel: Zoom in and out.
